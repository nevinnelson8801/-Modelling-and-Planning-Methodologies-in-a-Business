### 🔍 What is Smart VendAI?
Smart VendAI is an AI-powered vending machine solution that integrates **Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and IoT** to overcome the limitations of traditional vending machines — such as manual stock checks, unpredictable inventory, and poor customer insight.

---

### 🎯 Problem Being Solved
Traditional vending machines suffer from stockouts, overstocking, high labour costs, no real-time monitoring, and poor location-based product decisions. Smart VendAI automates and intelligently manages all of these.

---

### 🛠️ Methodologies Used
| Method | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Conceptual Modelling | Maps system components and data flow |
| Business Model Canvas | Validates commercial viability |
| System Dynamics (Insight Maker) | Simulates long-term inventory behaviour |
| Regression Analysis (Python) | Forecasts sales from foot traffic data |

---

### 📊 Key Findings
- A strong positive relationship was found between **foot traffic and units sold**, expressed as: `UnitsSold = 3.73 + 0.93 × FootTraffic` (coefficient: 0.92)
- Simulation revealed that current replenishment rates are **insufficient** for growing demand, recommending upward adjustment of reorder parameters.
- The Business Model Canvas confirmed the business is **commercially feasible and sustainable.**

---

### ✅ Conclusion
Smart VendAI is a **practical, scalable, and data-driven** solution. The combination of modelling, forecasting, and simulation confirms it can deliver reduced operational costs, fewer stockouts, and improved customer satisfaction across high-traffic locations like airports, universities, gyms, and hospitals.
